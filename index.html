About

P Bhanu Prakash, being a doctoral candidate is highly interested in research on Artificial Intelligence application in the field of Management, HR & Business Administration. He has a particular passion for looking at data from high-throughput technologies and using causal inference and graph-based tools. Besides Human resources and Marketing, he is curious about human data interaction that defines the flow of research, ways it impacts analysis, understanding, and scientific communication. He develops tools that help people to make data-driven decisions related to research.
